Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pocket12c handles information when you use the app.

Data Collection

Pocket12c does not collect personal information, account information, contact information, location data, or usage analytics.

Data Stored on Your Device

Pocket12c stores calculator state locally on your device so that your calculator memory, settings, and saved program state can persist between launches.

  • Calculator stack and registers
  • Display and formatting preferences
  • Theme and feedback settings
  • Program memory and related calculator state

Data Sharing

Pocket12c does not sell, share, transmit, or disclose your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Pocket12c does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or account systems.

Children's Privacy

Pocket12c does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@deerdawn.com.
